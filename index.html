About the Feastival

Gastro Feastival is Ghana's premier food and drink celebration, bringing together farmers, chefs, foodies, and innovators for a weekend of culinary exploration and cultural exchange.

This year's event coincides with Farmer's Day weekend, honoring the hardworking farmers who form the backbone of Ghana's food ecosystem. We're celebrating the journey from farm to fork with tastings, workshops, and demonstrations that showcase the best of Ghanaian cuisine.
